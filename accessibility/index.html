The Standard

Inclusion is intentional.

The objects we curate exist for the unhurried, the deliberate, the fully present. Our digital space should honour those same principles. A rest that requires effort to access is not a rest at all.

I. Visual Clarity

We target WCAG 2.1 Level AA contrast across all text and interactive elements. Our dark palette is designed for legibility — warm cream on near-black, measured at every breakpoint.

II. Motion & Sensory

All animations on this site respect the prefers-reduced-motion system setting. When that preference is detected, transitions halt and parallax effects are disabled entirely.

III. Keyboard Navigation

Every interactive element is reachable and operable by keyboard alone. Focus indicators are visually distinct — a gold outline that follows your position through the page.

IV. Assistive Technology

The site is built on semantic HTML5 with appropriate ARIA labelling. Decorative elements are marked aria-hidden. Screen reader text is preserved where visual content is animated away.

Feedback is a gift.

If you encounter any difficulty navigating this site, please tell us. Your perspective helps us build a space that holds everyone.
